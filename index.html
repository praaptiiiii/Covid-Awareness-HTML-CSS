KNOW YOUR SYMPTOMS

If you are experiencing any of the symptoms stated, rush to your nearest healthcare centre.

Symptoms may include:
  • Cold and cough
  • Runny nose
  • Body ache
  • Loss of smell or taste

HOW COVID SPREADS

Refrain from:

  • Going out unless necessary
  • Touching contaminated objects
  • Having physical contact with outsiders

PRECAUTIONS

Follow these steps to avoid getting infected:

  • Wash hands frequently
  • Wear a mask
  • Avoid contact with sick people
  • Cover you mouth when you sneeze
